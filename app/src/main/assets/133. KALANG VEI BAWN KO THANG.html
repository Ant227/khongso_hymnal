KALANG VEI BAWN KO THANG
( Low in the grave He lay) S.S. 152


  1. Kalang vei bawn ko thang Kaklut hly mih tun Rx let hüm haimih ming Malai Isu.

Cho: Kuam ko thang hai rx bei ky Ra-ui hrak ük bürbür ja pai ky Thük mihly hak kuam ko pü ja pai mih tun (Rx ra ky)2 Haleluja rx let ky.
