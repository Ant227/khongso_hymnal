KAHLOI MIH TUN MAH KRUI ONG JO

(All the way my Saviour leads me) S.S 522

  1. Kahloi mih tun mah krui ong jo Rai mih teing kalum jo bah Paroi mira bürbür hüm ja pai Ang hring mih phum-ang jok nang M’hma hmang mih phobai mikha Limla hrak-ip tawk kapü (Hualung ky ko hai pjatra mih Kalum prai mih tui ang ki)2.
