BERAM JUA

(While shepherds watched their flocks)S.S.33.,E.H. 30


  1. Beram jua si ly tüp beram Hruklong kacu cüm vei Tamuk hai Angel si kathxp Phum-ang si hly au-phung.
  1. Miva kram mih hrak-thuk hrak-hma Bong kramva nü keing va Kalum hüm hnai prai mih satang Kxp ceng kaham katüm.
  1. Kamai Davida namting long cü David ceng phrek kalawn Hring vei mih tun Malai Khrista hüm Kheu ky kai khü hau va.
  1. Miva katüm mih raca hüm Khuk ty van hnai hnak nü Jok kjia hai lung mih tüm tum va Lang ty mih cü ki ky.
  1. Mi hai kly cü pahlo hümmih Kalum mih hai py meng Rasa thang Angel si cawn ra Malai Tamuk pahlo.
  1. Tamuk cü kalum misi kham Kadei kajua nakhü Malai kaham tawn prai nakhü Peimih lap awt hly ky.
