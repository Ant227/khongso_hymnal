HALELUJA AMEN


  1. Hosana pei bürbür va Haleluja Amen Malai Isu hüm kheu ky Bekhlehem nam tum Hmat hai kly cü kavan mih ceng bür hüm pikling mih Kalum mihly mah awtva Hosana peikhü.

  1. Kahma mih novei lüng ky, tüp ang mih Malai cü Kavan ra-oi hly phau nang ky Bawn prei bürbür hüm thük mira kamngo long hai Kaloi hümmih tun hai ka-i kavan prei cü Malai Isu ne.

  1. Ky kük mih Zion Malai kahma mih hüm hau mih Rasa thang kai hümmih cü khang, ün mih noman Congkhang hai congkhang luk pü, Malai nakhü Amen Van nang miva si cü khang hümmih noman.
