TAMUK MAH KACXK KHA KAPUM HLY

(God instituted marriage) S.S. 650


  1. Tamuk mah kacxk kha kapum hüm py nang Sür mih kimsut vei hly hüm haimih Kasom kalom mihak kamlum hümmih po Hring mirük vei jo tüng hüm haimih.

Cho:Kalum prai mihak meng si txr iva Katai kahnau kapum miluk nü Kacu kha pü nui rasa thang kamtom khü Hring mih tare vei jojo hümmih.

  1. Kaku kanu kapa kham hai kai vitky Ju hüm kam khawt hüm Isu mah txr Mija cü ju hak hua pum hlük lawn vitky Kimsut kim-ai Tamuk vui vei ky.
  1. Tamuk mah sxkcai mih bong kaprai nakhü  Cami kawt mih kly Tamuk mah txr Lamuk hak bawn hai miktut pum hlük naky Ka i kavanra mah prui cai ky.
  1. Isu cü Hmat kung pliu miva si hüm van  Hmat kham hai hringra jok mitho nü Mija ju hak hua cü kaklai kavan ky Miva kly cü kadawn joja khü.
  1. Aw kapa Ün hai rut luk ing ap nang ky Mija kham vei hly nü krui ong bah Hnai nohnai mikha pü pum hlük vei nakhü Ün jok miva si jok tüng nakhü.
