TAMUK KAVAN KY PRAI    
 

  1. Tamuk kavan ky prai mih cü, Kaklang hly bawn prei bür nakhü, Malai hnong hnai prai mihly phau isi jo, Ma-nga tahi si kalum va.
Cho:
 Hring hümmih satang cü, Kaphat khü peimih kacu ky, Khristin bürbür si cü, Man hra jojo hümmih, Ün hai limla sür som jo dei ah.
  1. Hring hüm jok mih Malai mah cü, Prei bürbür long vei misi hüm, Au mihak hring mih cü jok jom hümmih hia, Khüm hring vei dungpxn hümmih hai.
  2. Ceng congkhang kaham py cup va, Malai kham kai hly hümmih hai, Beram tahi thük mih rx tüng bei hümmih, Kalum meng hly pahlo nakhü.
